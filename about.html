About Me

Hello! I am Bimala Amgai, a dedicated and experienced Senior Agency Manager at Nepal Life Company with over 20 years of experience in the life insurance industry. Since starting my career in 2005, I have worked tirelessly to help clients understand and achieve their financial goals through tailored life insurance solutions.

With a strong passion for building relationships, I have successfully served over 4000+ clients and built 1000+ agencies. My goal is to foster trust with my clients, ensuring they feel confident in their financial decisions.

I believe that life insurance is a crucial part of any financial plan, and I am committed to making this essential service accessible and understandable for everyone. In my free time, I enjoy talking with people and traveling, which enriches my understanding of diverse needs and perspectives.
